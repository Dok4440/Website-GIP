Mission and Vision

Because we know that modern schools cannot function without computers anymore, we offer the help you need to get started with laptops or desktops for every student. We offer our help to all Flemish primary and secondary schools.

Besides installing Windows 10 on student laptops, we offer a whole range of software included in our packages. Our goal is to make it easy for every student and teacher to get started with modern technology.

So my mission is to eventually give every Flemish student a laptop that is ready to be used in school. Studies show that students who can take notes on a computer learn more from the lesson than when they have to write everything down by hand.

Of course, we are not naive. We know that many students abuse the use of laptops and play games during class. Therefore, we offer help in blocking software (and/or websites) on the laptops.
